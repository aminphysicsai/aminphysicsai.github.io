Insaan apni zindagi me Electric Field ki tarah kaam karta hai….. Electric field wo jagah hoti hai jahan force lagti hai, jahan kuch hona hi hai……… Zindagi me ye field hoti hai majboori, bhook, ambition, dar, zimmedari, competition. ………. Insaan in fields ke beech move karta hai, react karta hai, fight karta hai, girta hai, uthta hai. Yeh sab present ka pressure hai….. Lekin jo cheez reh jaati hai, wo Potential hoti hai……. Potential koi force nahi hai wo memory hai………. Insaan ne kis jagah kya mehsoos kiya, kya socha, kya sacrifice kiya, kis ko hurt kiya, kis ko heal kiya …. yeh sab space me record ho jaata hai, jaise electric potential space me store hota hai……. Ap kisi kamre me jaate ho aur bina kisi wajah ke bhari lagta hai…. wo wahi potential hai… beeti hui emotions ka.. Insaan mar jaata hai, uska electric field khatam ho jaata hai ……. wo ab kisi ko push nahi karta. Lekin uski potential energy …yade, dard, love, ideas wo reh jaati hain. Isliye duniya force se nahi chalti, yaadon ke potential se chalti hai… Hamari ek sachchi baat, ek imandaar kaam, ek deep soch kisi future ke dil ko bina touch kiye bhi chhoo sakti hai…… Jaise charged shell ke andar field zero hota hai….. par potential zinda rehta hai waise hi insaan khud chup ho jaata hai, par uski kahani space me ghoomti rehti hai.
